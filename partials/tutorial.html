BDD Assistant Tutorial

Introduction

Welcome to the BDD Assistant Tutorial. Following sections will explain the main concepts in more details:

Navigation

The main purpose of the navigation menu is to provide quick links to major sections of the application as well as the information of the current location.

Browse Stories

Browse Stories dialog allows users to navigate directories and stories, create new directories, create new, open or delete an existing story.

Story Form

Story Form is a representation of an BDD story. It is divided into 3 parts: Name, Story and Scenarios.

Story Operations

Following operations are allowed: save, revert changes, delete and rename.

Single Story Runner

Single Story Runner is commonly used during the specification and development of some story. After scenarios are written, developers should use the BDD in the TDD fashion.

Browse Composites

Composites are convenient way to define new steps composed of one or more predefined steps. Further on, composites are grouped into composite classes.

Composites Form

Composites Form is a representation of an composites class. It is divided into 3 parts: Class Name, Composites and Composite Steps.

Composites Operations

Following operations are allowed: create new composites, update existing composites, revert changes and delete composites.
